Iran in Depth in 16 Days Tour

Iran in Depth in 16 Days Tour

Day 1

Location: Tehran
Arrival to Imam International Airport, meet an assist by your tour guide. Transfer to Hotel.
Overnight: Tehran

Day 2

Location: Tehran
Tehran palaces tour; includes: Golestan Palace, Saad Abad Palace and Niavaran Palace. Flight to Shiraz in the evening.
Overnight: Shiraz

Day 3

Location: Shiraz
Shiraz city walking- tour includes: Shahcheragh Holly Shrine, Jame Mosque, Nasir Almolk Mosque, Narenjestan House & Garden, Zinat Almolk House & Museum, Hafez and Saadi Tomb.
Overnight: Shiraz

Day 4

Location: Shiraz-Yazd (455 KM)

Drive to Yazd, on the way visiting Persepolis.
Persepolis tour; includes: Persepolis (Takht-e Jamshid) and Necropolis (Naqsh-e Rustam).
Overnight: Yazd

Day 5

Location: Yazd
Yazd city tour; includes: Jame Mosque, Water Museum, Amir Chakhmagh Complex, Zoroastrian Towers of Silence and the Fire Temple.
Overnight: Yazd

Day 6

Location: Yazd-Naein-Isfahan (313 KM)
Drive to Isfahan, on the way visiting Naein.
Naein tour; includes: Watching carpet manufacturing and traditional Pirnia House (Jame Mosque is optional).
Overnight: Isfahan

Day 7

Location: Isfahan
Isfahan city tour; includes: Naqsh-e Jahan Square, Imam Khomeini Mosque (former Shah Mosque), Sheikh Lofollah Mosque, Ali Qapu Palace, Chehelsotoon Palace, Hasht Behesht Palace & Garden.
Overnight: Isfahan

Day 8

Location: Isfahan-Abyaneh (172 KM)
Abyaneh your; includes: Meeting people with traditional and colorful costumes, getting familiar with Abyaneh intact architecture, old houses and wooden doors.
Overnight: Abyaneh

Day 9

Location: Kashan-Hamadan (401 KM)
Drive to Hamadan, on the way visiting Kashan.
Kashan tour; includes: Fin Gardens, Tabatabayis House, Broojerdis House, and Agha Bozorg Mosque & School.
Overnight: Hamadan

Day 10

Location: Hamadan
Hamadan city tour; includes: Baba Taher Tomb, Avecina Tomb, Alavian Dome, Hegmataneh Site & Museum, Tomb of Esther & Mordechai and Ganjnameh Inscriptions.
Overnight: Hamadan

Day 11 Location: Hamadan-Kermanshah (189 KM) Kermanshah city tour; includes: Tagh-e Bostan and Bisotun Complex and Anahita Temple. Overnight: Kermanshah

Day 12

Location: Kermanshah-Qazvin (419 KM)
Drive to Qazvin, on the way visiting one of the world’s longest water caves at Ali Sadr, and also Soltaniyeh Dome built by Mongolian Empire.
Overnight: Qazvin

Day 13

Location: Qazvin-Bandar Anzali (212 KM)
Qazvin city tour; includes: Visiting Alamut Castle, Old Caravanserais, and bazaar. Then drive to Bandar Anzali.
Overnight: Bandar Anzali

Day 14

Location: Bandar Anzali-Sarein (261 KM)
Eco tour; includes: Excursion to visit Siah Keshim Protected Area which is located at Bandar-e Anzali South West (60 KM) and Chamkhaleh where has one of the best Iran’s sand beaches, then drive to Sarien and visit Saein SPA.
Overnight: Sarein

Day 15

Location: Sarein-Ardebil-Tehran (624 KM)
Ardabil city tour; includes: Sheikh Safi Tomb, Anthropology Museum and Ardabil Historic Bazaar.
Flight to Tehran in the evening.
Overnight: Tehran

Day 16

Location: Tehran
Transfer to Imam International Airport for departure flight.
